About Us

Providing authentic Javanese hospitality experiences in Yogyakarta's most desirable locations.

Our Story

Madani Group Hospitality was founded with a vision to showcase the best of Yogyakarta's hospitality while providing guests with comfortable, authentic accommodation options. What began as a single property has grown into a collection of carefully curated homes, villas, and apartments across the region.

Our journey started when we recognized the need for quality accommodations that blend modern comfort with traditional Javanese elements. We wanted to create spaces where travelers could feel at home while experiencing the rich culture and natural beauty of Yogyakarta.

Today, we manage 15 properties throughout the Yogyakarta region, with 6 owned properties and 9 partner properties. Each location has been personally selected for its unique character, convenient location, and ability to provide an exceptional guest experience.

Our Philosophy

At Madani Group Hospitality, we believe that accommodation is more than just a place to sleep—it's an integral part of the travel experience. Our philosophy centers around three core principles:

Authentic Javanese Hospitality

We embrace the warm, welcoming spirit of Javanese culture in everything we do, from the design of our properties to the way we interact with our guests.

Comfort Meets Culture

Our properties blend modern amenities with traditional elements, creating spaces that are both comfortable and culturally enriching.

Personal Connection

We strive to create meaningful connections with our guests, offering personalized recommendations and services to enhance their stay in Yogyakarta.

Our Eco-Friendly Commitment

At Madani Group Hospitality, we are deeply committed to environmental sustainability and reducing our ecological footprint. We believe that responsible tourism and hospitality should contribute positively to both local communities and the environment.

Waste Management System

All our properties implement a comprehensive waste separation system with clearly labeled bins for organic waste, non-organic waste, and recyclable plastic bottles. This helps minimize landfill impact and promotes recycling.

Organic Waste Recycling

We maintain a small chicken farm that helps consume organic waste from our properties. The chicken manure and remaining organic matter are then transformed into compost, which we use in our gardens, creating a closed-loop system.

Local and Sustainable Products

Whenever possible, we source locally made products, from furniture to guest amenities, reducing transportation emissions while supporting local craftspeople and businesses.

By staying with us, you're not just experiencing authentic Javanese hospitality — you're participating in sustainable tourism that respects and preserves the local environment for future generations.
