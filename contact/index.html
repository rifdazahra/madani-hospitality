Frequently Asked Questions

How can I make a booking?

The easiest way to book is to contact us directly via WhatsApp. We'll check availability for your desired dates and guide you through the booking process.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our standard cancellation policy allows free cancellation up to 7 days before check-in. For cancellations made less than 7 days before arrival, a fee may apply. Please contact us for specific details related to your booking.

Do you offer airport pickup services?

Yes, we can arrange airport transfers for an additional fee. Please let us know your flight details in advance so we can organize this service for you.

Can you help with organizing tours in Yogyakarta?

Absolutely! We can recommend and help arrange tours to popular attractions around Yogyakarta. Just let us know what you're interested in seeing, and we'll assist with the arrangements.
